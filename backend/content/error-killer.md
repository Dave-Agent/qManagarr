qBittorrent marks torrents as errored when they encounter problems it cannot recover from on its own — missing files on disk, tracker rejections, or general client errors. These torrents consume queue space and will never complete without manual intervention. Left unattended they pile up, and Sonarr and Radarr won't know to search for an alternative unless the torrent is removed.

This module removes errored torrents automatically so your *arr apps can detect the failure and kick off a new search. You can control exactly which states trigger removal: **error** covers general failures, **missingFiles** handles cases where previously downloaded data has disappeared from disk, and **unregistered** handles torrents whose tracker no longer recognises them — common on private trackers when an upload is removed from the site.
