When a torrent finishes downloading, qBittorrent keeps it in the queue for seeding. For *arr stack users this is largely unnecessary — Sonarr and Radarr manage the media library directly, and once the files have been imported the torrent's job is done. Leaving hundreds of completed torrents sitting in the queue makes it harder to see what's actively downloading and wastes resources tracking dead swarms.

This module removes completed torrents (and their source files from the download directory) automatically after a configurable delay. Set the delay long enough that your *arr apps have had time to import the files before the torrent disappears — 3 hours is a safe default for most setups, but on slower systems or with very large files you may want to increase this. If you notice imported media occasionally going missing, your delay is too short.
